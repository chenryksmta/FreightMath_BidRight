The Fuel Surcharge Reality

Breakthrough Fuel — Threat or Managed Input?

~35% of RFPs now mandate Breakthrough Fuel Recovery. Carriers who treat FSC as a black box lose margin. FreightMath + BidRight turn it into a controlled variable.

The Carrier Risk
  • Rate compression — FSC isolated, procurement pressure shifts entirely to linehaul
  • MPG squeeze — Moving benchmarks from 6.0 to 6.5 MPG cuts reimbursed gallons ~8% per load
  • Uncompensated costs — Deadhead, detention & idling never reimbursed under Fuel Recovery
  • Black-box pricing — Proprietary algorithms carriers cannot independently verify
How FM + BidRight Protect You
  • Cost-plus linehaul — FM calculates total required price (LH + FSC), then subtracts shipper-specific FSC to set your true floor
  • Uncompensated fuel recovery — Deadhead MPG, idle burn & detention costs built into every lane price
  • Volatility premium — FSC shortfall vs. historical DOE pricing automatically factored in
  • BidRight enforcement — Pre-RFP due diligence checklist, protective contract language & audit-ready documentation
