## SLIDE 14: Solution #3c — Area Control

**Visual:** Area Control map/interface.

**Key features:**
- Select and manage freight areas across the country
- Factors in driver home locations, HOS qualifications, daily mileage targets
- Auto-applies to every new bid; can refresh on past bids
- Surfaces the 60 target lanes out of a 10,000-lane RFP automatically

*Speaker Notes:*
Area Control lets you define what your ideal network looks like — including where your drivers live, their hours of service, and daily mileage. It automatically surfaces the lanes you want out of massive RFPs. Seconds to import tens of thousands of lanes, seconds to evaluate which ones fit your network.
